YOU CAME FROM

"WAY 45 conveys Rafael Leão as a person — his feelings, his life story, his lessons."

Born in Almada, raised in Seixal — postal code 2845 — Rafael Leão carries his roots in his name. WAY is the path he chose; 45 is the neighbourhood that shaped him.

Signed to 608 Records, selected as the MTV PUSH Artist of July 2023. Two albums. One voice. Unmistakably his own.
