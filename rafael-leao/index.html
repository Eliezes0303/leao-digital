AC
MILAN

Since arriving at San Siro in 2019, Rafael Leão has transformed from a raw prospect into one of the most electrifying forwards on the planet. His explosive pace, razor-sharp close control and instinct for the decisive moment have made him the engine of the Rossoneri attack.

Wearing the iconic #10 shirt, Leão reached 50 Serie A goals in November 2024 — cementing his legacy as one of the most lethal forwards in modern Italian football.

PORTUGAL

Born in Almada, Portugal, Leão has become one of the nation’s most important footballers. From winning the U17 European Championship in 2016 to lifting the UEFA Nations League in 2025, his international career mirrors his club trajectory — relentlessly upward.

At the 2022 World Cup he scored against Ghana and Switzerland, announcing himself on football’s grandest stage and cementing his place as a key starter for the Seleção das Quinas.

BEYOND

WAY 45

Rafael Leão is more than a footballer. Under the alias WAY 45, he is a recording artist. “45 is the postal code of the neighbourhood where I grew up. It means never forgetting.” Through music he expresses the person behind the player.

In 2024 he published Smile, his autobiography — an intimate account of the journey from Almada to San Siro, published by Piemme.
